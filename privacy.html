🐱 Privacy Policy - EnglishQuest Kids

Last updated: June 10, 2026

1. Introduction

EnglishQuest Kids ("the App") is a free English learning game for children, available on the Pi Network ecosystem. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the following information:

  • Pi Username: Used only to display your name in the game. We do not store this on any server.
  • Game Progress: Stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit game data.

3. How We Use Information

We use your Pi username solely to personalize your in-game experience. We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the Pi username required for authentication via Pi SDK.

5. Pi Network Authentication

Login is handled exclusively through Pi Network's SDK. We do not store passwords or sensitive credentials. Please refer to Pi Network's own Privacy Policy for details on how Pi handles your data.

6. Data Security

All game data remains on your device. We do not operate servers that store user data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
